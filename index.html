Bucket List

  1. Keawaula

    May 20, 2018

    Got this one in the dead of winter. It was packed with locals. All good and friendly. Can't wait to go back again.

  2. Makaha

    Feb 7, 2019

    Possibly the best beach on the west shore. A long drive, but well worth it for the serf and sand quality. Very few tourists.

  3. Nanakuli

    Nov 30, 2021

    Incredible offshore break, but completely rock covered bed. You also need to climb down some serious cliffs to get in the water. Pro level only!
